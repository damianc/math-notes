# T Helpers (Th)

| Subset | Main Cytokines Produced | Main Function | Key Transcription Factor | Driven by (Inducing Cytokines) |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------|---------------------------|---------------------------------|
| **Th1** | IFN-γ, IL-2 | cellular immunity; activates macrophages; defense against intracellular pathogens | T-bet | IL-12, IFN-γ |
| **Th2** | IL-4, IL-5, IL-13 | anti-parasitic immunity; allergy; IgE class switching | GATA3 | IL-4 |
| **Th17** | IL-17A/F, IL-22 | neutrophil recruitment; defense against extracellular bacteria and fungi | RORγt | IL-6, IL-23, TGF-β |
| **Tfh** | IL-21 | B-cell help in germinal centers; high-affinity antibody production | Bcl-6 | IL-6, IL-21 |
| **Treg** | IL-10, TGF-β, IL-35 | suppresses immune responses; maintains tolerance | FoxP3 | TGF-β, IL-2 |
| **Th9** | IL-9 | anti-helminth immunity; mucosal immunity; allergy | PU.1 | IL-4 + TGF-β |
| **Th22** | IL-22 | epithelial and skin barrier defense | AHR | IL-6, TNF |
| **ThGM** | GM-CSF | autoimmunity; neuroinflammation (e.g., MS) | (heterogeneous/unclear) | IL-23 |
| **Th3** | TGF-β | oral/mucosal tolerance | FoxP3 (often) | TGF-β |
| **Thαβ (Tr1-like)** | IL-10 | antiviral regulation; immune suppression | (uncertain) | IFN-α/β |
