# Integration by Parts: Exercise 1

$$
\large
\int 2x \ e^x \ dx
$$

## Parts

$$
\int u \ dv = uv - \int v \ du
$$

$$
\begin{array}{lcl}
u=2x && du=2
\\
dv=e^x && v=e^x
\end{array}
$$

## Solution

$$
\begin{array}{rl}
2xe^x - \displaystyle\int 2e^x \ dx & = 2xe^x - 2 \displaystyle\int e^x \ dx
\\
& = 2xe^x - 2e^x
\\
& = 2e^x(x-1)
\end{array}
$$

## Result

$$
\int 2x \ e^x \ dx = 2e^x(x-1)
$$
