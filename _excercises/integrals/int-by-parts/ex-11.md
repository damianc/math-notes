# Integration by Parts: Exercise 11

$$
\large
\int \frac{x}{e^{-x}} \ dx = \int xe^x \ dx
$$

## Parts

$$
\int u \ dv = uv - \int v \ du
$$

$$
\begin{array}{lcl}
u = x && du = 1
\\
dv = e^x && v = e^x
\end{array}
$$

## Solution

$$
\begin{array}{rl}
xe^x - \displaystyle\int e^x \ dx & = xe^x - e^x
\\
& = (x-1)e^x
\end{array}
$$

## Result

$$
\int \frac{x}{e^{-x}} \ dx = (x-1)e^x
$$
