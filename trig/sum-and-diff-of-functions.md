# Sum and Difference of Functions

## The same angle

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\cos \alpha + \sin \alpha & = \sqrt{2} \sin(45°+\alpha)
\\
& = \sqrt{2} \cos(45°-\alpha)
\\
\\
\cos \alpha - \sin \alpha & = \sqrt{2} \cos(45°+\alpha)
\\
& = \sqrt{2} \sin(45°-\alpha)
\\
\\
\sin \alpha - \cos \alpha & = -(\cos \alpha - \sin \alpha)
\\
& = -\sqrt{2} \cos(45°+\alpha)
\\
& = \sqrt{2} \sin(\alpha - 45°)
\end{array}
$$
