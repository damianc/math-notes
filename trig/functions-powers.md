# Trigonometric Functions Power

## Power of 2

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\sin^2(\theta) & = 1-\cos^2(\theta)
\\
& = \frac{1}{2}[1-\cos(2\theta)]
\\
\\
\cos^2(\theta) & = 1-\sin^2(\theta)
\\
& = \frac{1}{2}[\cos(2\theta)+1]
\end{array}
$$

## Power of 3

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\sin^3(\theta) & =
\frac{1}{4}[3 \sin(\theta) - \sin(3\theta)]
\\
\\
\cos^3(\theta) & =
\frac{1}{4}[3 \cos(\theta) + \cos(3\theta)]
\end{array}
$$
