# Fresnel Integrals

$$
\begin{array}{rcl}
S(u) & = &
\displaystyle
\int_0^u \sin(x^2)\ dx
\\
\\
C(u) & = &
\displaystyle
\int_0^u \cos(x^2)\ dx
\end{array}
$$

## Normalized Fresnel Integrals

$$
\begin{array}{rcl}
S(u) & = &
\displaystyle
\int_0^u \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2} x^2\right)\ dx
\\
\\
C(u) & = &
\displaystyle
\int_0^u \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2} x^2\right)\ dx
\end{array}
$$
