# Integrals of Exponential Functions

| $\boldsymbol{f(x)}$ | $\boldsymbol{\int f(x)\ dx}$ | $\boldsymbol{f(x)}$ | $\boldsymbol{\int f(x)\ dx}$ |
|--|--|--|--|
| $$e^x$$ | $$e^x$$ | $$a^x$$ | $$\frac{a^x}{\ln(a)}$$ |
