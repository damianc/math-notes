# Binomial Expansion

$$
\large
(a+b)^n = \sum_{k=0}^n
\binom{n}{k} a^{n-k} b^k
$$

$$
{\color{#666}\text{where }}
\tiny
\binom{n}{k} =
\frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{rcl}
\boldsymbol{(a+b)^2} & = &
a^2+2ab+b^2
\\
\\
\boldsymbol{(a+b)^3} & = &
a^3+3a^2b\ +
\\
& & b^3 + 3ab^2
\\
\\
\boldsymbol{(a+b)^4} & = &
a^4+4a^3b\ +
\\
& & 6a^2b^2\ +
\\
& & b^4 + 4ab^3
\\
\\
\boldsymbol{(a+b)^5} & = &
a^5 + 5a^4b + 10a^3b^2\ +
\\
& & b^5 + 5ab^4 + 10a^2b^3
\end{array}
$$
