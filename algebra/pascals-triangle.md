# Pascal's Triangle

$$
\tiny
\begin{array}{ccccccccc}
&&&&&& \binom{0}{0} &&&&&& \\
&&&&& \binom{1}{0} && \binom{1}{1} &&&&& \\
&&&& \binom{2}{0} && \binom{2}{1} && \binom{2}{2} &&&& \\
&&& \binom{3}{0} && \binom{3}{1} && \binom{3}{2} && \binom{3}{3} &&& \\
&& \binom{4}{0} && \binom{4}{1} && \binom{4}{2} && \binom{4}{3} && \binom{4}{4}
\\
&...&...&...&...&...&...&...&...&...&...&...
\end{array}
$$
