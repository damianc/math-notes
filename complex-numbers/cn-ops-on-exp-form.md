# Operations on Complex Numbers in Exponential Form

## Multiplication

$$
\begin{array}{l}
z_1 = r_1 e^{i \phi_1}
\\
z_2 = r_2 e^{i \phi_2}
\\
\ 
\\
z_1 z_2 = r_1 r_2 e^{i(\phi_1 + \phi_2)}
\end{array}
$$

## Division

$$
\begin{array}{l}
z_1 = r_1 e^{i \phi_1}
\\
z_2 = r_2 e^{i \phi_2}
\\
\ 
\\
\frac{z_1}{z_2} = \frac{r_1}{r_2} e^{
 i(\phi_1 - \phi_2)
}
\end{array}
$$

## Exponentiation

$$
z^n = (re^{i\phi})^n = r^n e^{ni\phi}
$$

## Taking _n-th_ Roots

$$
z_k = \sqrt[n]{r} e^{
 i \frac{2k\pi + \phi}{n}
}
$$

$$
\small
k \in \langle 0,n-1 \rangle \cap \Bbb{Z}
$$
