# Imaginary Unit

$$
\large
i = \sqrt{-1}
$$

## Square

$$
\large
i^2 = -1
$$

for example:

$$
\begin{array}{rl}
(2-2i)i & = 2i-2i^2
\\
& = 2i-(-2)
\\
& = 2i+2
\\
& = 2(i+1)
\\
& = 2(1+i)
\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{rl}
4i(1-2i) & = 4i-8i^2
\\
& = 4i-(-8)
\\
& = 4i+8
\\
& = 4(i+2)
\\
& = 4(2+i)
\end{array}
$$

## Negative Root

$$
\large
\sqrt{-k} = \sqrt{k} \sqrt{-1} = \sqrt{k} \ i
$$

for example:

$$
\sqrt{-4} = \sqrt{4} \sqrt{-1} = 2i
$$

$$
\sqrt{-\frac{1}{4}} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{4}} \sqrt{-1} = \frac{1}{2} i = \frac{i}{2}
$$
