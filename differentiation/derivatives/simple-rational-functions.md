# Derivatives of Simple Rational Functions

> $$\frac{d}{dx}\left[\frac{f(x)}{k}\right] = \frac{1}{k}\left[\frac{d}{dx} f(x)\right]$$

## Monomials

| $\boldsymbol{f(x)}$ | $\boldsymbol{\frac{d}{dx} f(x)}$ |
|--|--|
| $$\frac{k}{ax}$$ | $$-\frac{k}{ax^2}$$ |
| $$\frac{k}{ax^2}$$ | $$-\frac{2k}{ax^3}$$ |
